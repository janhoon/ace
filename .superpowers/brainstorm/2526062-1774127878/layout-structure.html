Key Layout Decisions

Click to confirm or flag items you want to change

1

Full sidebar (240px) collapses to 0px

Toggle with Cmd+B. No icon rail — fully hidden when collapsed. Content reclaims full width.

2

AI Command Bar fixed at bottom of every view

Persistent Cmd+K bar for natural language queries, dashboard generation, and contextual AI. Expands into a chat panel when active.

3

Tonal layering instead of borders

Following the "No-Line Rule" — surface hierarchy (#0d0e10 → #121316 → #1e2022 → #2b2c2f) defines structure. Ghost borders only where accessibility requires it.

4

High-density "flight deck" aesthetic

Compact spacing, monospace metrics, small labels, sparklines. Optimized for information density, not whitespace.
