B: Mission Control

More structured — active item gets a left accent bar, flyout has a header with close button, inline search per section, favorites + recents. Slightly heavier but more feature-rich. Drop shadow on the flyout gives it panel depth. Feels like a proper cockpit.

  • Ace logo mark (amber) at top of rail
  • Hover-to-peek, click-to-pin flyout
  • User avatar at bottom with popover menu
  • Kinetic warm palette (amber primary, warm neutrals)
  • Settings icon pinned to bottom of rail
